Welcome!

This is Jeopardy! Staying Alive.

I'm your host Alex Trebek.

JSA is a single player trivia game based on the popular game show of which I am also the host. You will start out with $1000 and the goal is to answer as many questions as possible before your money runs out. Each question is assigned a value based on it's difficulty. A correct answer is awarded cash equal to the questions value while an incorrect response is penalized in like fashion.

Good Luck!
